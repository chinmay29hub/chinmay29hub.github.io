Frequently Asked Questions

Is my CV also available for download in Word?

Due to the complexity of our templates, it is unfortunately not possible to download the CV in Word.

Can I download my resume for free?

Yes, you can download your resume for free in .PDF format unlike any other Resume builders.

How long should a resume be?

From research it has been found that most of the recruiters recommend using maximum one page resume, especially when you are a student or having less than 5 years of relevant work experience.

Would my resume be made public?

No way! When you build your resume with us, it is accessible only to you. Your information stays private and for your eyes only.

Is it OK to lie on your resume?

No, it is not OK to lie on your resume. If the employer finds out, your application will be rejected immediately. Lying on your resume is not only dishonest, but social media and new technology make it easier than ever for employers to fact check your documents. If you’re hired because of a lie on your resume, you may lose your position and damage your professional networking opportunities.
